The Honest Promise

AI will not make your wedding perfect, and it will never replace the professionals you hire. What it gives you is clarity: knowing what to do next, with a draft already in front of you.

Your florist still knows flowers. Your photographer still knows light. The admin in between is where an AI assistant earns its place, and it is available at 3am when the question will not wait. Every draft it produces is yours to review, and every number is yours to verify.

About

The bride behind the brand

I got married in 2025: 120 guests over two days, speaking three languages between them, with no coordinator. I sewed my civil ceremony dress over eight months. My dad and I built the lawn games from scratch. And when the admin threatened to swallow the joy of it all, I taught an AI assistant to help me carry it.

More than 80 tasks later, I knew the system was worth sharing. I build the tools and the guides, and I teach the method live in the Power Hour. The AI assistant is the part that never sleeps.

I keep my face and name offline on purpose. The work speaks for itself.
